👥 Clans

Form alliances, coordinate attacks, and dominate the world together.

What Are Clans?

Clans are player-created groups for teamwork and social gameplay. Members share a clan chat, coordinate attacks, and can recruit together. Clans enable large-scale warfare impossible solo.

Clan chat: Private messaging visible to all clan members.

Roles: Leader, Co-leader, Elder, Member (hierarchical permissions).

Recruitment: Direct DM invites; or public applications and voting.

Max members: No hard limit; grows as more players join.

Creating a Clan

To create a clan, you need:

Cost: 1000 Food, 1000 Timber, 1000 Gold, 1000 Metal
(A steep investment, so plan ahead!)

Founder benefit: You become the clan Leader automatically.
This gives you full control: recruiting, roles, management.

How to create: Go to the Clan tab (/clan). Click "Create Clan". Enter a name and click confirm.

Joining a Clan

Three ways to join a clan:

1️⃣ Public Applications

View all clans in the clan list (/clan). Click "Apply" on a clan card. Your application is pending until an Elder or above accepts it.

2️⃣ Recruitment DM

A clan Elder or above can send you a recruitment DM from the world map. Click "Accept" to join instantly (no approval needed).

3️⃣ Public Clan Pages

Visit a public clan page (/clan/<id>). Click "Apply" if not a member yet. Same as method 1, but you can see the clan description and member list first.

Roles & Hierarchy

Clans use a 4-tier hierarchy. Higher roles have more permissions:

Role Permissions Can Manage
👑 Leader Full control Co-leaders, Elders, Members; transfer leadership; disband clan
🔱 Co-leader Near full Elders, Members; accept/reject applications; send recruits
📜 Elder Moderation Accept/reject applications; send recruitment DMs
👤 Member Chat only Talk in clan chat; can leave

All new joiners start as Members. Leaders promote based on trust and activity.

Clan Features

💬 Clan Chat

Real-time messaging with all members. Synced via 3-second polling. Perfect for coordinating attacks or just hanging out.

📋 Members Tab

View all members with their roles. Leaders can promote/demote or kick members.

📝 Applications Tab

(Elders+) View pending join requests. Accept or reject with one click.

📜 Clan Info

Clan description (editable by Leader/Co-leader). Shows founding date and member count.

Leaving & Disbanding

Leaving a Clan

Any non-Leader can leave instantly by clicking "Leave" in the clan header.
If you're the Leader and others remain, you must transfer leadership first.

Disbanding a Clan

If the Leader is the last member, leaving automatically disbands the clan. All clan data (chat, messages, applications) is deleted.

⚠️ Careful! Disbanding is permanent. You lose all clan history.

Clan Strategy Tips

  • ✓ Join early. Coordinate with clanmates to conquer territory faster.
  • ✓ Communicate constantly. Use clan chat to share targets, warnings, and strategies.
  • ✓ Coordinate attacks. Hit the same enemy fort with multiple armies for guaranteed capture.
  • ✓ Defend each other. Help members recapture lost forts.
  • ✓ Recruit strategically. Balance new players with veterans.
  • ✓ Share resources. Wealthier members can gift or support newer players.
  • ✓ Trust your leadership. A strong Leader makes or breaks a clan.
