👹 Monster Camps

A reliable source of loot for growing your economy.

What Are Monster Camps?

Monster camps are temporary, non-territorial targets scattered across the map. They appear as purple dots. They contain small monster garrisons and offer Gold and Metal loot when defeated.

Purpose: Practice combat. Generate early-game resources.

Respawn: After you defeat a camp, a new camp spawns elsewhere on the map.

Ownership: No one owns camps. Anyone can attack any camp.

Garrison: 2–30 monsters (depending on difficulty).

Spawn Mechanics

Spawning: The map maintains ~10 active monster camps at all times. When you defeat a camp, a new one respawns elsewhere.

Difficulty distribution: When a new camp spawns, its difficulty is randomly rolled using the percentages shown in the table above.

Location: Camps spawn in random empty cells across the map.

When do they spawn?: When the server checks map occupancy (usually every time you refresh the map or periodically).

Loot & Rewards

Defeating a camp gives you Gold and Metal:

Current rule: Camp victories award fixed Gold and Metal loot.

Loot is added directly to your castle's resources. No pickup required.

Monster Camp Strategy

  • ✓ Start small. Your first 5 camps should all be 1-star. Build confidence.
  • ✓ Grind for resources. Running 1-2 star camps repeatedly is fast and safe.
  • ✓ Save travel time. Nearby camps = quick trips = rapid resource accumulation.
  • ✓ Upgrade armies between runs. Once you have resources, train more troops and tackle higher stars.
  • ✓ Monitor spawn patterns. If you see a cluster of 2-star camps, focus that area.
  • ✓ Use as economy filler. While building troops, hit camps to keep resources flowing.
