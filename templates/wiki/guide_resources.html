💰 Resources & Economy

Master the four resources and understand how to build your economy efficiently.

The Four Resources

Every action in BattleCells costs resources. You manage four types:

🌾 Food

Used for training troops, building some structures.

Starting: 200

Produced by: Farm (0.05/s, level 1)

🪵 Timber

Used for buildings, military structures, and upgrades.

Starting: 200

Produced by: Lumber Mill (0.05/s, level 1)

💛 Gold

Used for tech, recruitment, and premium structures.

Starting: 100

Produced by: Merchant (0.05/s, level 1)

⚙️ Metal

Used for defense buildings, weapons, and fortifications.

Starting: 100

Produced by: Mine (0.05/s, level 1)

How Resource Production Works

Production buildings generate resources automatically based on elapsed time. The system uses lazy evaluation: production is calculated only when you collect.

Production Formula

amount = base_rate × 2^(level-1) × elapsed_seconds

Example: A level 2 Farm

Farm base rate: 0.05 Food/s
Level 2 multiplier: 2^(2-1) = 2
Effective rate: 0.05 × 2 = 0.1 Food/s

After 100 seconds: 0.1 × 100 = 10 Food

💡 Key insight: Upgrading a building from level 1 to 2 doubles its output. Level 3 = 4× output. This exponential growth is huge for long-term economy.

Collection Strategy

Resources are only collected when you manually click Collect in the resource panel. Strategic collection timing can optimize your economy:

  • Frequent collection (every 1–5 minutes): Keeps resources flowing; less total resources accumulated but enables rapid spending.
  • Delayed collection (every 10+ minutes): Massive resource bursts; good for planning big builds or attacks at specific times.
  • Before logging off : Always collect before leaving so you don't lose passive production time.

Scaling: Multiple Locations

As you progress, you'll capture forts. Each fort can have its own buildings and armies:

Castle: Primary economy. Build 5–6 production buildings + military buildings.

Fort 1: Secondary army base. Build Garrison + Stable; 2–3 production buildings for overflow.

Fort 2+: Dedicated military barracks or forward operating bases.

Resources pool across all your locations — you can spend gold from any location on any construction.

Pro Economy Tips

  • ✓ Upgrade your farms early. Level 2 farm = double output. Massive compounding gain.
  • ✓ Balanced production matters. A bottleneck in one resource slows everything.
  • ✓ Watch your spending. Build troops gradually; don't bankrupt yourself.
  • ✓ Collect before long journeys. Resources keep generating while you're logged off (if you collect right before leaving).
  • ✓ Plan your upgrades. Going from level 1→2 is worth more than building an extra level-1 building.
