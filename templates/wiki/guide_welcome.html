🌍 Welcome to BattleCells

A turn-based strategy game where you command armies, build fortresses, and compete for territory.

What is BattleCells?

BattleCells is a real-time strategy game played on a 50×50 world map. You start with a castle, build resource-producing facilities, train armies, and expand your influence by attacking forts and claiming territory.

The game combines economic management (farming resources) with tactical combat (positioning units, predicting outcomes) and social gameplay (forming clans with other players).

The Core Loop

💰

1. Build & Harvest

Place production buildings (farms, mills, mines) to generate resources over time.

⚔

2. Train & Recruit

Spend resources to train soldiers and recruit them into your army.

🎯

3. Explore & Attack

Scout the world map for targets (monster camps, forts, other players) and dispatch armies to attack.

🏆

4. Claim & Defend

Capture forts to own territory. Build defenses to protect what's yours from rivals.

Key Concepts

🏰 Your Castle

Your home base. It cannot be destroyed or attacked. You build resource and military buildings at your castle to produce troops.

🗺 The World Map

A 50×50 grid with three types of entities: your castle (blue), your forts (green), enemy forts (red), monster camps (purple), and unowned monster forts (orange).

💪 Armies

Groups of trained soldiers. You command them to attack targets on the map. Combat is deterministic — the stronger army usually wins.

⚡ Travel Time

Armies take time to travel across the map. Closer targets arrive faster. You can see the countdown and resolve the attack when it arrives.

🎖 Clans

Player-created groups with shared chat, applications, and social features. Form alliances with other players to coordinate attacks and defense.

What You Can Achieve

  • ✓ Build an economic powerhouse producing vast resources
  • ✓ Train a powerful army and claim territory from monsters and other players
  • ✓ Fortify captured territory with defense buildings and troops
  • ✓ Form or lead a clan and coordinate with dozens of other players
  • ✓ Discover advanced strategies and optimize your army composition
  • ✓ Participate in large-scale warfare as part of a clan
